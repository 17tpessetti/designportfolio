Intestina Logo Creation and Hi-Fi Mockup

The Intestina logo and hi-fi design project was one of the most intricate and content-heavy projects I completed. We were given a fictional client (Intestina) who wanted us to create a logo for their new app. We were challenged with creating a full logo outline with a color palette design explanation, and size/type variants. Also, we were asked to include our logos with images and merchandise in order to make sure it was scalable and able to be used universally.
